Services

At Sunland Trucking LLC, we will ensure that the transportation of goods is done in a safe and timely manner. We take our job very serious and want our customers to feel confident knowing that their shipments are handled with the utmost care and professionalism. We prioritize reliability, efficiency, and open communication, ensuring that every delivery reaches its destination securely and on schedule.

General Freight

  • General freight refers to non-specialized cargo that does not require special handling, refrigeration, or hazardous material permits.
  • Typically includes consumer goods and packaged products.

Beverages

  • Transportation of non-refrigerated bottled, canned, or packaged drinks, including water, soda, juice, alcohol, and energy drinks.
  • Requires careful handling to prevent damage.
